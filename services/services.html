Services

Under Interior

Trapdoor services, believe in designing spaces that work for you. Each project is unique in requirements and our in-house team enjoys creating a custom designed space that completely fits your requirement. We cover False Ceiling, Painting Flooring, wall tiling, Plumbing, Electrical wiring, wall cladding, wall panel, plywood work, glass fitting, all work under interior and We provide all these services to other company commercial spaces like offices, malls, showrooms, startup.

Under HVAC & Cooling system

Our team at trapdoor are experts in providing Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning services for our projects. The system shall maintain the Temperature, Humidity, Air Flow and the pressure of the area. A properly designed system will provide fresh outdoor air for making a comfortable indoor environment and easy year round maintenance.

Fire system

In any project, the most important part is the work of the fire system. We at Trapdoor carry out any type of fire work required by the project. Which will maintain the fire alarm and fire sprinkler. These alarms can be activated automatically from smoke detectors and heat detectors or can also be activated through manual fire alarm activation.
